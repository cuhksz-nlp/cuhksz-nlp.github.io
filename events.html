We organize a reading group (weekly in most cases) in each semester to discuss the recent advances in NLP and its related areas. One or two papers will be presented in each meeting. We will also have a discussion section after each presentation to discuss about what is presented. Everybody is welcome!

The time for the meeting will be normally announced at the beginning of each semester. Please check this page regularly for the next topics/papers to be presented and the location of the meeting.
